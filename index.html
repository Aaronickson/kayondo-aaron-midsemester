Welcome to Wilden Primary school

We are delighted to welcome you to the official website of Wilden Primary School, a place where education meets inspiration. At Wilden Primary School, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds, fostering creativity, and instilling lifelong values. Our school is a vibrant hub of learning and growth, located in the heart of Wakiso, Bulenga. Whether you’re a prospective parent, a proud student, or an esteemed visitor, we invite you to explore all that our school has to offer. Through this website, you can learn more about our rich history, diverse services, exciting activities, and the warm, inclusive community that makes Wilden Primary School truly special. Thank you for visiting our website—together, let’s shape a brighter future!

About Us

Welcome Message

Welcome to Wilden Primary School, a cornerstone of academic excellence and holistic development located in Wakiso, Bulenga. Our school was established in 2002 to provide quality education. Since its establishment, Wilden Primary School has been committed to nurturing young minds, fostering creativity, and empowering students to reach their fullest potential.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution that inspires and prepares students for a brighter and successful future through quality education and strong values.

Our Mission

At Wilden Primary School, we aim to provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where every child is encouraged to thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all that we do.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior.
  • Respect: Fostering an inclusive environment that values diversity and mutual respect.
  • Teamwork: Encouraging collaboration among students, teachers, and parents.

Our Legacy

Located in the heart of Bulenga, Wilden Primary School has a rich history of academic success, extracurricular achievements, and community involvement. Over the years, we have built a reputation as a school that not only prioritizes education but also cultivates lifelong skills, talents, and strong moral character.
